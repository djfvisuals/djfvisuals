David J. Franco is a passionate and results-driven graphic designer with a strong foundation in digital marketing and branding. His creative journey began at a young age through music and art, eventually leading him to discover graphic design at 16. After earning a bachelor’s degree in Graphic Design from Florida International University, he launched his professional career, working as a digital marketing specialist and designer at Miami Alkaline Water and a production specialist/designer, before securing a role at Valuetainment, the number one business podcast in America, founded by Patrick Bet-David.

With a deep understanding of design, branding, and visual storytelling, David specializes in creating high-impact designs that enhance brand identity and engage audiences. His ability to merge aesthetics with marketing strategy allows him to develop visuals that are not only compelling but also drive measurable results. Whether working on digital campaigns, brand development, or multimedia content, David is committed to delivering innovative and effective design solutions.
